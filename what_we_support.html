Supported Technologies

As AGA strives to be at the forefront of technology and innovation, we need to create a cutoff point for the types of devices that we can support. Using updated technologies helps keep your information safe and your experiences seamless. With that being said, AGA is only able to support technologies that the creator themselves are able to support. Below is a list which corresponds with Auth0 (our authentication system's supported requirements), Apple, Google and Microsoft. Some legacy browsers and devices may still work fine however, they are not guaranteed to function properly, and it is highly recommended that you upgrade your devices and continue to do so on a regular basis.
