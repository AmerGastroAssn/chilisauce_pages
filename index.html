Product Theater

Product Theaters are turnkey, 45-minute marketing presentations about your company’s products or services. These sessions do not offer CME credit; rather, they give exhibitors the freedom to create a presentation centered on their company’s products.

Learn More

Satellite Symposia

Satellite Symposia (SS) provide your organization with a unique opportunity to host educational programs before or after DDW scientific sessions, often offering CME credit to participants. A limited number of symposia are permitted so be sure to reserve yours today.

Learn More

In-Conjunction With (ICW) Meetings

ICWs are meetings and events arranged by exhibitors, third party planners, educational institutions or other non-profit organizations during DDW. These ancillary meetings are held at participating hotels and other venues in the host city.
