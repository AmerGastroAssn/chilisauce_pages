The AGA welcomes our corporate partners to be members of our corporate roundtable. The roundtable engages corporate magnates and AGA leadership in dialogue regarding innovations in practice, clinical and scientific advancements, policy, and other issues that impact the science and practice of gastroenterology and hepatology.

By harnessing the best minds in these areas truly can we understand the state-of-the-science accomplishments that are being achieved and leverage them to better serve the gastroenterological community and improve care for patients.

Membership Information

Membership to the corporate roundtable support programs that foster scientific discovery and maximize funds for research. Benefits of participation in the roundtable span the following areas:

  • Access to AGA leadership and other industry leaders
  • Up to date information on the latest news in the field
  • Opportunity to share your insights and opinions
  • Visibility at DDW events
  • Recognition in AGA communications including journals, website and newsletters

Roundtable members at all levels are invited to participate in annual joint meetings. These meetings provide a unique opportunity for interaction within the GI community, enabling members to share information with each other and hear speakers address common topics of interest.

Fees will support the AGA Research Foundation.
